Meet Amanda

As a native of chicago, I have always loved fine arts.
I grew up loving to draw and create fashionable items
that were a little before its time. While attending
cosmetology school I realized that I had an undiscovered
passion for nails and nail art. After awhile of perfecting
my craft I began to incorporate fashion trends into
my nail art. This led me to further pursue my first love
which is fashion. I believe art is how you visually express
yourself. It show how one feels and what you want
people to perceive you. Fashion, is an extension of how
you greet others. Embracing your style can boost your
confidence, and when you feel better you become
more productive.


It’s said that we dream in black & white.
Well i choose to dream in color.

The first time I ever styled someone from head to toe my
client was going through alot. I offered and said “let's do
a makeover”. She saw her face beat, new hair cut & color,
outfit , and just cried. At that moment I knew this was how
I can heal people. I knew it was morethen just playing
dress up. It was like we changed how she dreamed…
No more black and white. We only dream in color.

“It’s said that we dream in black and white.

Well I choose to dream in color.”-Amanda Pye
